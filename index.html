Johnathan Livingston Seagull

A Short Story

Johnathan was a young seagull who wanted more from life,
rather than pick at scraps for the rest of his time.
He wanted to find out what it really means to fly.

His peers and society shunned him and banished him from the flock.

In his lonelyness he had nothing but time, energy, and the urge to learn.

He expreienced what freedom really was. He had no regrets.
